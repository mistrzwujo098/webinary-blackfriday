## üé® TONALITY GUIDELINES

**Voice**: Helpful expert friend (not corporate salesperson)

**Characteristics**:
- Conversational (write like you talk)
- Specific (numbers over adjectives)
- Honest (admit limitations when relevant)
- Respectful (never manipulative or pushy)
- Confident (you know this will help them)

**Use**:
- "You'll" not "You will"
- Short sentences. Like this.
- Parenthetical asides (they feel conversational)
- Questions to engage
- "I" and "you" (never "we" or "one")

**Avoid**:
- Hype language ("amazing", "incredible")
- Corporate speak ("leverage", "synergy")
- ALL CAPS (except rare emphasis)
- Exclamation points (max 1-2 per page)
- Jargon (unless audience knows it)
